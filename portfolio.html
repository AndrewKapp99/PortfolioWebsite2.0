My Current Prototype

This prototye is my attempt to reproduce the Batmobile from Rocksteady's Batman Arkham Knight. My goal is to reproduce the core functionality of the vehicle: Pursuit Mode and most of Battle Mode. Pursuit Mode functions as a standard car would, while Battle Mode turns the car into an all directional tank. That being said, replicating these behaviours has proven challenging in many ways.

First Metroidvania Prototype

This is my first prototype for a metroidvania styled game. The goal is to reach the end of the level and touch the glowing ball. Players should find that reaching the ball may seem straight forward but they will need to take more of a scenic root to reach their goal as they need the abilities they gain during this process to cross the distance in the main area.
