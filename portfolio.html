First Metroidvania Prototype

This is my first prototype for a metroidvania styled game. The goal is to reach the end of the level and touch the gloaing ball. Players should find that reaching the ball may seem straight forward but they will need to take more of a scenic root to reach their goal as they need the abilities they gain during this process to cross the distance in the main area.
