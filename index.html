About Me

My name is Andrew Kapp, I am a student at the University of the Witswatersrand in Johannesburg, South Africa. I am doing a Bachelors of Engineering Sciences in Digital Arts.

I enjoy playing video games in my free time and I am in the process of learning to create my own projects as well. I mostly side with Programming and creating mechanics I have seen before but have recently found a joy in thinking of patchwork projects from the games I have played or the ones I am playing currently.

I have the goal to work as a Game Developer for intenationally recognized companies such as Guerilla Games, CD Projekt, or Warner Brothers in the hopes that I can work on rojects similar to my favorite games such as Horizon Zero Dawn, The Witcher franchise, and the Batman Arkham games which have all made significant contributions to my vision and hopes for the future.
